# Smart City Complaint System - Citizen Workflow

Here is the step-by-step guide on how a citizen can use the platform to report and track an issue.

## Step 1: Access the Dashboard
First, log in to your account and go to the **Citizen Home** dashboard. Here you will see your profile greeting and the complaint submission form.

## Step 2: Start a New Complaint (Details)
Scroll to the **"📝 New Complaint"** section. You will start on Step 1 (Details).
1. **Enter a Title**: Type a clear title for the issue in the "Issue Title" box (e.g., "Broken Streetlight").
2. **Upload Images**: Drag and drop photos of the issue into the designated area, or click it to browse and select images from your device.
3. **Generate AI Description**: Once your images are uploaded, click the **"image- analyses"** button. The AI will scan your images and automatically write a detailed description of the issue for you.
4. Click the **"Next"** button.

## Step 3: Select the Category
After clicking Next, you will be taken to Step 2 (Category).
1. Review the grid of available categories:
   - 🚧 Pothole
   - 🗑️ Garbage
   - 💡 Streetlight
   - 💧 Water Supply
   - 🚽 Sewage
   - ⚡ Electricity
   - 🔊 Noise
   - 📋 Other
2. **Click on the category card** that matches your issue.
3. Click the **"Next"** button.

## Step 4: Provide Location Details
After clicking Next, you will be on Step 3 (Location).
1. **Enter Address**: Type the exact location or landmark of the issue in the "Location / Address" box.
2. **Enter Ward/Area**: Type your ward number or city zone.
3. **Add Tags (Optional)**: Add any relevant tags like "urgent" or "safety hazard".
4. Finally, click the **"🚀 Submit Complaint"** button.

## Step 5: Receive Confirmation
Once submitted, you will immediately see a green **Success Card** at the top of your dashboard.
- It will display your unique **Complaint ID**.
- It will also show the **Priority Level** that the AI automatically assigned to your issue (e.g., High, Medium, Low).

## Step 6: Track Your Complaint
To check on the progress of your issue, go to the **History** section in your app. 
The status of your complaint will change sequentially over time:
1. **Pending**: Submitted and waiting for the authority to review.
2. **Assigned**: The authority has assigned a field officer to your case.
3. **In-Progress**: The officer is currently working on fixing the issue.
4. **Resolved**: The issue has been fixed successfully.

## Services Overview
Our Smart City platform allows citizens to request resolution for a wide range of civic issues. The current services supported include:
- **🚧 Road Infrastructure**: Reporting potholes, road damage, and craters.
- **🗑️ Waste Management**: Issues related to garbage collection and public waste bins.
- **💡 Street Lighting**: Reporting broken, flickering, or missing streetlights.
- **💧 Water Supply**: Interruptions in clean water supply or pipe leakages.
- **🚽 Sewage & Drainage**: Blocked drains, overflowing sewage, and related health hazards.
- **⚡ Electricity & Power**: Unscheduled power outages or dangerous electrical infrastructure.
- **🔊 Noise Pollution**: Reporting public disturbances and unauthorized loudspeakers.
- **📋 Other Civic Issues**: Any other city-related complaint not covered above.

## Contact & Support
If you face any issues using the platform, or your complaint is not being addressed within a reasonable timeframe, you can reach out to our dedicated support team.

- **📞 Direct Support Helpline**: At any time, you can click the **"📞 Call Support"** button located at the bottom right corner of your screen (or call **+91 9782100977** directly).
- **Escalations**: High-priority AI-flagged complaints are automatically escalated if not resolved within 48 hours. If you wish to manually follow up, please keep your **Complaint ID** handy when contacting support.
